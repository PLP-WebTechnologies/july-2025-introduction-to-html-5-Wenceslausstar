What is HTML5?

HTML5 is the latest version of the HyperText Markup Language. It introduces new semantic elements that improve both accessibility and search engine optimization.

Understanding Accessibility

Web accessibility ensures that websites are usable by people with disabilities. Using semantic tags like <header>, <nav>, and <main> helps screen readers navigate your site effectively.

Why It Matters

Accessible websites reach a broader audience and comply with legal standards. They also provide a better experience for everyone.
